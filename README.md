# 🤖 ME3

### Your personal AI assistant, email, calendar, website, and plugins. Hosted by you.

**Ready to take ownership of your digital life? ME too!**

ME3 provides a home to the parts of your digital life that should belong to you.

![ME3 screenshot](apps/web/public/me3.gif)

note: some features may require Cloudflare’s $5/month plan.

## ☁️ Install

All you need is a GitHub and Cloudflare account, and the nerve to press the button.

[![Deploy to Cloudflare](https://deploy.workers.cloudflare.com/button)](https://deploy.workers.cloudflare.com/?url=https://github.com/soulinkfoundation/ME3)

## 🤔 Why ME3?

Because... 
- Your AI, inbox, calendar, and website should not all belong to someone else.
- And you shouldn't need to spend a weekend in the terminal to get a PAI running. 

## 🖥️ What's in ME3

- **🤖 An AI Assistant** you create jobs for that will save you time.
- **🚀 Mission Control** where you manage your projects.
- **🌐 A ME3 site/profile**: is like a digital business card built for humans and AI agents.
- **📧 Email** for you or your 🤖, whatever will allow you to check email WAY LESS.
- **📆 A Calendar** your 🤖 can use to help you spend more time with the humans that matter to you.
- **🧩 Plugins**: you can build, extend and modify to make ME3 your own.

## 📜 Docs

Be honest, you were never going to read them, so ME3 ate them all. Once you're set up, you can ask ME3 what you need to know, like how to configure a custom domain, run the app locally, create your own plugin.
